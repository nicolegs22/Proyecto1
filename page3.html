Lyrics & Style of The Neighbourhood

About Their Style

The Neighbourhood is known for their distinctive dark and atmospheric sound. Their music combines elements of alternative rock, pop, and R&B, often exploring themes of love, loss, and identity.

One of their most recognized songs, "Sweater Weather", helped define their sound and gave them international recognition. Their lyrics often have a melancholic tone, which resonates with a broad audience.

Popular Lyrics

From the song "Sweater Weather":

"'Cause it's too cold for you here and now, so let me hold both your hands in the holes of my sweater."

This line has become iconic, symbolizing the band's ability to blend warmth and isolation in their lyrics.

Common Themes in Their Music

  • Love and relationships
  • Identity and self-discovery
  • Isolation and loneliness
