Studio Albums

I Love You. (2013)

This is the debut studio album by The Neighbourhood, released on April 22, 2013. It features their hit single "Sweater Weather".

Track Highlights: Sweater Weather, Afraid, Let It Go

Wiped Out! (2015)

The second studio album, released on October 30, 2015. It solidifies their unique sound with dreamy atmospheres and deep lyrics.

Track Highlights: R.I.P. 2 My Youth, Prey, Cry Baby

Hard to Imagine The Neighbourhood Ever Changing (2018)

This is a compilation album that merges EP releases with new material, showing the evolution of their style.

Track Highlights: Scary Love, Softcore, Void

"Softcore" es el quinto tema del tercer álbum de estudio de The Neighbourhood. El tema combina un ritmo electrónico contundente con sintetizadores new wave de los 80 para crear una canción de pop alternativo pegadiza.

Compas - My Favorite Song

Compas es una de las canciones más personales de la banda, destacando por su letra introspectiva y melódica.

Frase destacada: "We want to believe in something, but we're stuck between dreams and reality."

Jesse Rutherford, el cantante principal de la banda, mencionó en una entrevista: "La música es nuestra forma de escapar, pero también nuestro reflejo de lo que somos".
