About The Neighbourhood

The Neighbourhood is an American alternative rock band formed in Newbury Park, California, in 2011. The band is known for its moody and atmospheric sound, blending genres like rock, pop, and R&B.

Their most well-known song is "Sweater Weather", which brought them international fame. The band has since released several albums that explore dark, introspective themes with a unique musical style.
